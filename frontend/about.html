The Beginning

From a Dream to a Movement

ASKR Clothing was founded in 2020 by a group of fashion enthusiasts who believed that premium quality clothing shouldn't be a luxury for the few — it should be accessible to everyone who dares to be bold.

Starting from a small studio in Bangalore, we've grown into a nationwide brand serving over 50,000 customers across 500+ cities in India. Our Black and Orange identity isn't just colors — it's a statement: bold, confident, and unapologetically premium.
